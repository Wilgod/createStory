## YouTube 頻道圖標 (Channel Icon)

### 頻道圖標 `@圖標` — 白色比熊犬拳擊冠軍（圓形圖標版）

**適用工具**: Midjourney / Flux / DALL-E / Ideogram  
**建議尺寸**: 800×800px（正方形，YouTube 會自動裁切成圓形）

```
A fluffy white Bichon Frise dog standing upright like a human in a triumphant boxing champion pose, both front paws raised high holding white boxing gloves in victory, wearing white boxing shorts with a glittering gold championship belt around the waist, large round black eyes shining with joy, extremely fluffy and well-groomed white coat, wide confident grin, dramatic overhead spotlight from above casting a heroic glow, packed boxing arena crowd cheering in the blurred background with bokeh lights, close-up portrait framing from chest up, perfectly centered composition, vibrant and punchy colors, bold cinematic lighting, photorealistic, 8K quality, square 1:1 aspect ratio, no text, no watermark.
```

**關鍵設計選擇**:
- 勝利姿勢（雙拳高舉）比戰鬥姿勢更適合圖標，觀眾第一眼感受到「贏家」氣場
- 胸部以上特寫 → 圓形裁切後臉部清晰，小尺寸下仍辨識度高
- 金色冠軍腰帶 → 增加視覺焦點和「頻道主角」身份感
- 散景背景燈光 → 保持背景豐富但不干擾主角

---

**備選版本（極簡背景）**:

```
A fluffy white Bichon Frise dog standing upright like a human, both front paws raised high holding white boxing gloves in a V-for-victory pose, wearing white boxing shorts with a gold championship belt, large round joyful black eyes, extremely fluffy well-groomed white coat, triumphant and cheerful expression, centered composition chest-up portrait, clean deep navy blue gradient background, dramatic single overhead spotlight, bold vivid lighting, photorealistic, 8K, square 1:1 aspect ratio, no text, no watermark.
```

**備選版本用途**: 深藍背景版在 YouTube 頻道頁面上對比度更強，更易辨識。
